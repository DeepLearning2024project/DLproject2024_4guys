%	（等最后写完论文，再写摘要）对于寿命较长的电子原件，其寿命可达10年甚至20年，我们不可能实实在在地检测其寿命，直到其真正损坏。经济的做法是对其寿命进行预测。在本文中，我们利用收集的一类微波电子管的、其电流的时间序列数据，预测其寿命情况。我们使用LSTM、TCN、GRU、RNN、MLP作为Batchmark，探究Autoformer模型以及Timesnet模型在此类问题上的性能，并尝试做出改进。

for electronic originals with long life, their life can reach 10 or even 20 years, and we cannot actually detect their life until they are really damaged. The economic way to do this is to make predictions about its lifespan. In this paper, we predict the lifetime of a kind of microwave tube by using the time series data of its current. We use LSTM \cite{10.1162/neco.1997.9.8.1735}, TCN\cite{DBLP:journals/corr/abs-1803-01271}, GRU\cite{DBLP:journals/corr/ChungGCB14}, RNN\cite{DBLP:journals/corr/ZarembaSV14}, MLP\cite{Zeng_Chen_Zhang_Xu_2023} as Batchmark, explore the performance of Autoformer\cite{NEURIPS2021_bcc0d400} model and Timesnet\cite{DBLP:journals/corr/abs-2210-02186} model on such problems, and try to make improvements.
\end{abstract}
